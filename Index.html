Amazon Logistics - Operations Supervisor 2015-2017

During my time as an Operations Supervisor for Amazon Logistics my time here was split between night shift and day shift which involved two very different operations.

Night Shift: On night shift my team ran the sortation operation from start to end. We received our deliveries from various fulfilment centres to be sorted for onward movement to delivery centres to be sent out to the customer. From Receiving deliveries, sorting pallets and loading/departing trailers for onward delivery. This was heavily dependant on hitting times to ensure the goods met customer delivery promise.

Day Shift: We worked with various Delivery service providers to ensure goods were departed and delivered to customers on time. This involved monitoring delivery drivers, chasing DSPs promptly with any issues, sending support to struggling drivers to ensure no failed deliveries. I dealt with customer complaints and would review these and provide feedback to DSPs to improve customer experience.
